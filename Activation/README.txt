----------------------------------------------------------------------

SECURITY & PREREQUISITES:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Execution Context: Requires Administrator privileges.
- Execution Policy: May require setting the execution policy to 
  Bypass or RemoteSigned.
- Internet Connection: [Yes/No]

INSTRUCTIONS:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open PowerShell with Run as Administrator.
2. Open the `activation.txt` file.
3. Copy the entire contents of `activation.txt`.
4. Paste the script into the elevated PowerShell window.
5. Press Enter and follow any on-screen instructions until the process is complete.

Note: Ensure PowerShell is running with administrator privileges; otherwise, the activation process may fail.
