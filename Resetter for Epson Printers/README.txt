==================================================
EPSON PRINTER RESETTER (ADJUSTMENT PROGRAM) GUIDE
==================================================

IMPORTANT PRE-REQUISITES (BEFORE DOING ANYTHING):
--------------------------------------------------
1. PASSWORD:
   The resetter utility executable (Adjprog.exe) requires a password to open.
   Password: i-loadzone.com

2. ANTIVIRUS PROTECTION:
   You MUST temporarily turn off Windows Defender / Antivirus real-time protection.
   Why? Adjustment programs (Adjprog.exe) use low-level printer APIs and are frequently
   falsely flagged as threats and auto-deleted by security software.

--------------------------------------------------
INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE:
--------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Disable real-time antivirus protection.
Step 2: Extract "Epson Resetter.zip".
Step 3: Open the extracted folder, right-click "Adjprog.exe", select "Run as administrator", and enter the password "i-loadzone.com" when prompted to access the program.
Step 4: Click the "Select" button at the top:
        - Choose your printer Model Name from the dropdown list.
        - Click "OK".
Step 5: Click the "Particular adjustment mode" button.
Step 6: From the list of options, select "Waste ink pad counter" and click "OK".
Step 7: Check the box next to "Main pad counter" (and "Platen pad counter" if available).
Step 8: Click the "Check" button to read the printer's current counter status.
Step 9: With the boxes still checked, click the "Initialize" button to reset the counter to 0.
Step 10: Click "OK" on any prompts, turn off your printer, and click "OK" again.
Step 11: Turn your printer back on. The red blinking lights should be gone, and the printer should be ready for use.
Step 12: Turn your antivirus protection back on.
